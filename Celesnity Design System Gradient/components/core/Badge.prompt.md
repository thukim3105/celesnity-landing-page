**Badge** — small status, category, or metadata pill. Use `gradient` sparingly for a single hero highlight; tonal variants for quiet labels.

```jsx
<Badge tone="gradient">Live</Badge>
<Badge tone="cobalt">Clear skies</Badge>
<Badge tone="neutral" size="sm">v1.0</Badge>
```

Tones map to the accent spine (`cobalt` · `violet` · `magenta`) at low opacity, plus `neutral` glass and `gradient`.
